Yo' Grandma's Killer Kimchi Fried Rice

Despite the title, I do not own a Korean grandma, especially one that teaches the art of frying rice, it is just very yummy in the tummy.

Ingredients

  • Kimchi (real fermented to get the juice)
  • Leftover fried rice (must be that day old fridge rice)
  • Minced garlic
  • Green onion
  • Spam
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • One perfect large egg
  • Optional: Onion, Mushrooms, Cheese

The Step-by-Step

  1. Heat a frying pan on medium heat and add oil
  2. Add minced garlic and green onion
  3. When fragrant add kimchi and make sure you get all the kimchi juice in there (other veggies go here too should you choose to add them)
  4. Add the sliced spam to the concotion
  5. Add rice once everything is cooked (add gochujang if there is not enough kimchi juice)
  6. Add 1 tbsp of oyster sauce and sesame oil
  7. Coat until it looks pretty similar to the signature red of kimchi fried rice
  8. Cook a sunny side egg and add it to the top
  9. Bon Appetit!
