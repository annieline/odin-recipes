A Very Rosy Tteokbokki

I really enjoy pasta and korean food, so this is a combo of both!

Ingredients

  • Tteokbokki
  • Fishcake
  • Minced garlic
  • Sausage or spam
  • Tomato paste
  • Gochujang
  • Gochugaru
  • Curry powder
  • Heavy cream
  • One perfect large egg
  • Optional: Onion, Mushrooms, Cheese

The Step-by-Step

  1. Defrost frozen tteok or fishcake by putting it in a bowl of lukewarm water
  2. Heat a frying pan on medium heat and add oil
  3. Add minced or sliced garlic
  4. When fragrant add onion, mushroom, whatever you want to include
  5. Add meats
  6. Once meat is browned, add a cup of heavy cream and milk
  7. Add 1 teaspoon of curry powder, tablespoon of gochujang and gochugaru
  8. Mix until its lookin' saucyy
  9. -I recommend you boil the tteok and fishcake slightly before you add it into the pan-
  10. Include in the pan and stir until cooked thoroughly
  11. Add a sunny side egg or soft boiled egg for a gooey eggy sauce, yum!
  12. Bon Appetit!
